# mushroom-pepperfry

mushroom pepperfry is a very tasty and yummy and a healthy dish and it will keep u energetic!

So to make this yummy dish you will need:
## Ingredients:
 1. mushroom(1packet)
 2. 1 Big Onion
 3. 1 Big tomato
 4. 1 green chilli
 5. 1 tsp pepper
 6. 1/2 tsp turmeric
 7. salt to taste
 8. 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
 9. 1 tablespoon oil(cooking oil)
